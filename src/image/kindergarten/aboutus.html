We are a trained group
of teachers with a passion to educate

Our staff has over 10 years of experience working with children. These are qualified teachers and educators who are trained in all the subtleties of working with children from infancy to preschool.
Senior preschool age is an important and responsible period in the life of a child. Therefore, our team has its own approach to each child individually. Our main goal is to develop in children the most important life competencies, which are designed to facilitate the adaptation of the child in society, to introduce him to the social and cultural values of his people, to educate an active, thinking, respectful citizen, but also able to defend his own interests.

love and a special approach
to each child

It is very important to give love and care to the child not only from the parents.
After all, in this way the qualities of a sensual and attentive person can be laid in a child. our team creates favorable conditions for each child, giving him the maximum attention and time that each child needs individually.

a learning method with which your child will love to learn new things

The importance of education is an integral part of education, and of course, our responsibility.
Our methods are among the best, as they are based on well-known specialists, teachers and psychologists. Your child will grow and develop properly.

time for a break and fun

Well, how about without interruptions and fun pastime.
Rest is necessary not only for adults, but also for children. And rest should be right. Especially for this, we have different competitions, events, also an animator in different cartoon costumes that children love so much.

constant safety of children in a
controlled area

We know how concerned you are for the safety of your child.
That is why our territory is fully controlled by CCTV cameras, security guards at the exit from the yard and at the entrance to the kindergarten building. Your children will never be out of control.
